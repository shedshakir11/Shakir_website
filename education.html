Education

My educational journey started early at Pra Jais (age 5–6), where I learned reading, writing, and social skills. This early stage sparked my curiosity and love for learning.

From ages 7–12, I attended Sekolah Kebangsaan Kanchong Darat, building stronger academic skills, making friends, and developing interests in science and arts.

From 13–18, I studied at Sekolah Agama Menengah Unwanus Saadah, focusing on religious studies and general subjects, learning values, ethics, and community involvement.

Currently, I study at UiTM Kampus Rembau, pursuing fitness, sports, and digital content, gaining knowledge in communication, nutrition, and media.

Overall, each stage blends academics with personal interests like riding, gaming, and fitness, shaping me for future goals.
